About Me

Steven Andrews

Let me introduce myself to you! I am living in Provo Utah, having been here for almost There years following my return from the Florida Jacksonville Mission. I decided to move to Utah from my home state of California so that I could be with friends and have an opportunity to go to school.

I am seeking my first degree in Applied Technology. I am so excited to be on this journey and to do so at an institution that reflects my values. Thus far, I've enjoyed the classes and the time that I've spent learning new skills. I look forward to continuing my education and doing so through BYUI!

I currently work as a forklift driver for LiveView Technologies, and am looking forward to expanding my skillset while I go to school and by seeking new opportunities in the tech space.

Between work, school, and my church calling I don't have lots of free time, but with the time I have I enjoy building things in my garage and getting outdoors for sports and recreational activities.
