Steven Andrews | WDD 130

Hello! My name is Steven Andrews and I am from Huntington Beach, California. I enjoy drumming and listening to music. My favorite bands are The Beatles, The Doors, The Rolling Stones, and Led Zeppelin. I am the youngest of four children, and I love my family very much. I hope to one day become a data analyst for a large company, and be able to provide for a family of my own. I served a full-time mission in the Florida Jacksonville Mission from 2020-2022. Serving during Covid-19 was certainly interesting, but I wouldn't trade that experience for anything in the world. I served about half my time in northern Florida, while the other half was spent in southern Georgia.

Utah

Utah is a state in the western United States. It is bordered by Colorado to the east, Wyoming to the northeast, Idaho to the north, Arizona to the south, and Nevada to the west. It also touches a corner of New Mexico in the Southeast. The state is a center of transportation, education, information technology, and research, and a major tourist destination for outdoor recreation. Salt Lake City, the state capital and largest city, is home to the headquarters of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (LDS Church).
