'Rapids' White Water Rafting

History

In the early 1980s, amidst the untamed beauty of the Rockies, Rapids White Water Rafting emerged from the fearless exploits of river guide Steven Andrews and his adventurous crew. Their passion for navigating the Colorado River's turbulent waters ignited a spark that led to the founding of the company in 1985. From its inception, Rapids White Water Rafting prioritized safety and environmental conservation, earning a reputation for excellence in the industry. Guided by top-notch experts and equipped with state-of-the-art gear, the company expanded its operations to encompass thrilling river routes across the nation. As the new millennium dawned, Rapids White Water Rafting embraced technological innovations, while remaining true to its core values. Today, it stands as a beacon of adventure, inspiring countless adventurers to experience the rush of conquering nature's fury while fostering a deep connection with the wilderness.
